The Global Shapers Community

  • Is an initiative of the World Economic Forum and the youngest stakeholders of the Forum. The World Economic Forum (WEF) was established in 1971 as a not-for-profit foundation and is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland. It is independent, impartial and not tied to any special interests. Its purpose is to empower global leaders to shape the future for the better. It was this institution that in 2011 created the Global Shapers Community, a network of young leaders between the ages of 18 and 27 years who are exceptional in their potential, their achievements and their drive to make a contribution to their communities.

  • Is a network of young people driving dialogue, action and change. Our hub draws together young leaders to act in non-partisan ways to impact the community based on local and global needs.

  • Is about apolitical dialogue with all of our local and global stakeholders.

  • Is focused on multi-stakeholder action .

  • Is working on Long term change.
