Our stand

2031 vision

Timișoara will be a dynamic smart city, a hub of transnational leadership, innovation and sustainability.
Timișoara will be the place to be at the national and Eastern Europe level for community and international opportunity.
A model for how to do self-sustaining community building and of interaction between the different ecosystems present within the region.

Our organization empowers young leaders, while easing access and collaboration between institutions, organizations, firms and other relevant actors in order to provide the best possible conditions for the local community to thrive and sustain itself for the long term. We collect and interconnect global opportunities for local needs.
Our activities adress comprehensive target audiences, focusing on diversity and inclusion, creating the space for diffrent stakeholders to work together for the betterment of our community.
We uphold integrity, freedom of speech, association and research.
We are respectful & inclusive.
We are collaborative and we do co-creation.
We are non-partisan and apolitical.
We are proactive and embrace experimentation and growing by doing and learning from mistakes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to some of the questions you may have.

  • What is the Global Shapers Community?

    The Global Shapers Community is a network of young leaders between the ages of 18 and 27 years who are exceptional in their potential, their achievements and their drive to make a contribution to their communities.

    The Global Shapers Community is an initiative of the World Economic Forum and the youngest stakeholders of the Forum.

  • Who are the Global Shapers?

    Global Shapers are a group of highly selective young extraordinary individuals with great potential for future leadership roles in society and share a spirit of entrepreneurship in the global public interest.

  • How can I join the Timisoara Hub?

    We welcome energetic young people to join our hub. Our hub recruitment cycle opens every August with a call for applications. All applications are reviewed by a recruitment committee set up by the Hub. Applicants who show promise age invited for an interview and successful applicants are extended an invitation to join the Hub. Announcement will be made via our social media platforms.

  • Do you pay membership dues?

    No. At the moment we are trying to remove all fees for our shapers and trying to fund ourselves through other sources.

  • How many Hubs are in Romania?

    There are two (2) hubs in Romania: Timisoara & Bucharest.
